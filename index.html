Glass

Improve the interaction of your mettings by blending your webcam and your screen.

Shortcuts

  • Enter/Exit Fullscreen: Ctrl+Alt+F
  • Show/Hide: Ctrl+Alt+H
  • Decrease opacity level: Ctrl+Alt+1
  • Increase opacity level: Ctrl+Alt+2
  • Exit: Ctrl+Alt+Q
